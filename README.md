# 🐾 TailyRam

> Where pets become influencers. 🚀

TailyRam is a next-gen social media app built for pets and their humans. Whether it's a lazy cat, chaotic dog, or exotic companion — this is their stage.

---

## 🧠 What is TailyRam?

Think Instagram… but **everyone is a pet.**

* No boring content ❌
* Just paws, chaos, and cuteness 🐶🐱

---

## ⚡ Core Features

* 🐾 **Pet Identity System**
  Every pet gets their own digital persona.

* 📸 **Content Feed**
  Scroll through endless adorable chaos.

* ❤️ **Engagement**
  Like, comment, and hype up other pets.

* 👥 **Follow System**
  Build your pet’s fanbase.

* 🔍 **Discover Mode**
  Find trending and viral pets.

---

## 🎯 Mission

To dominate the internet… but with pets.
